About Us

Our Mission

Founded in 2023 by Mikaia, Exploratium is a unique blend of platform/adventure and quiz game that promises hours of entertainment while encouraging players to explore, learn, and have fun.

The Game

Explore, Collect, and Conquer!
Embark on an exciting journey through three captivating maps: Street Roads, Forest Side, and Under the Sea. Take control of your character, collect valuable waste to earn points, and face off against formidable minions on your quest to defeat the ultimate boss of each map.

Knowledge is Power!
In Exploratium, answering questions is your key to victory. With questions of varying levels, your answers determine the damage dealt to the enemy and the points you earn. Choose wisely!

Game Pad

The Challenge

Keep an eye on your hearts! Lose them by:

  • Losing collected garbage (Lose half a heart).
  • Bumping into minions (Lose half a heart per bump).
  • Answering incorrectly against the final boss (Lose one entire heart).

What Sets Us Apart

At Exploratium, we're not just a game, but a community. Engage with fellow players, share tips, and join discussions on our forums. Stay connected through social media and be a part of the journey!

As we move forward, we aim to expand the Exploratium universe with exciting updates, new maps, and interactive features. Your feedback is invaluable, and we're committed to making your experience even better.
